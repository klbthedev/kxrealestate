Real Estate

The Odoo Real Estate module is a comprehensive property management solution designed to streamline the operations of real estate businesses. It provides tools for managing property listings, tracking leases, handling tenant communications, scheduling property maintenance, and generating financial reports.

Overview

In this module, you can list properties for sale or rent, manage bookings, and generate invoices. It also helps you analyze and manage both owner and rental contracts, making it easier to keep track of property transactions and agreements.

Features

Community & Enterprise Support

Available in Odoo 17.0 Community and Enterprise.

Property Listings

This feature allows you to manage and organize a list of all properties available for sale or rent. You can include important details such as the property’s location, size, price, and type (e.g., residential or commercial). It helps you keep everything in one place, making it easier to find and update property information.

Property Booking

The Odoo Real Estate module allows you to manage property building information and handle property bookings. After a property is booked, you can easily create contracts for both ownership and rental purposes.

Sell and Rental Agreements

This feature allows you can create and manage contracts for both selling and renting properties. It covers all the essential terms, contract durations, and payment schedules. This ensures that every deal is well-documented and legally sound, making the process of selling or renting out properties smoother and more efficient.

Invoicing

The invoicing feature automatically generates invoices for property sales or rental payments. It ensures that every financial transaction is recorded accurately and that your clients receive their invoices on time. This helps in maintaining clear and professional financial records.

Payment Tracking

This feature allows you to track all payments related to property sales and rentals. You can monitor payments that have been received, track any outstanding amounts, and send reminders for overdue payments. It’s a simple way to keep your finances in check and avoid any missed payments.

Property Report Analysis

The Odoo Real Estate module enables you to generate reports for ownership installments and analyze ownership contracts. This helps you keep track of payment schedules and contract details, ensuring smooth management of property sales and ownership agreements.

Customer Management

This feature helps you keep track of all your clients, whether they are buyers, sellers, landlords, or tenants. You can store their contact details, preferences, and any interactions you’ve had with them. It’s a great way to build and maintain strong relationships with your clients, ensuring you understand their needs and can provide better service.

Document Management

This feature allows you to store and manage all important documents, such as sales contracts and rental agreements. It ensures that all your legal and financial documents are organized and easily accessible, reducing the risk of losing any critical information.
